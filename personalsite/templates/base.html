About Me

Do what you love & the rest will come
-Dennis Crowley


There is a special feeling the moment you are able to create something with a little bit of code and a little bit of hardware. There are no real words to describe this euphoria, but once you understand it, you can understand where I am coming from.


The main reason I majored in computer engineering was because I wanted to know how embedded system worked. It was as simple as that. I have spent many hours not just in school learning about embedded systems, but have dedicated a huge chunk of my own personal time learning about embedded systems. I am in no way a master of this art, but I am in no way a novice.


From my previous internship at TeraDiode, I had the wonderful opportunity to explore very high level thinking through machine vision which was very new and different for me. In addition, I worked exclusively on higher-level languages including VB.NET and C#. Working with such a high-level language for a summer at a company has given me a deeper appreciation for higher-level languages.


This deeper appreciation has led to reach out to broader endeavors not limited to embedded systems to see what else there is out there in the world for me.



On the side, I am an avid music buff. I have recently endeavored on to learning to play the guitar and learning the ins and outs of music production through Ableton.
